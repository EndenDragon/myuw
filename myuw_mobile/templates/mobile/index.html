Your Tegrity Courses

Welcome...

Tegrity Manager makes presentation capture and review available to all UW faculty, staff, and students and gives them the ability to use Tegrity across disciplines, sections, departments.

Tegrity originated for classroom use and its terminology reflects its origins. Because you may be using Tegrity for purposes other than teaching, here are some quick definitions to avoid confusion.

Course: a container of classes. To keep your recordings organized and easy to find, you will probably want to group related classes under the same course.

Class: one or more recordings (video, audio, PowerPoint slides, etc. ) made on the same day. Multiple classes appear as thumbnails in the class list. Think of multiple classes like chapters in a book.

Instructor: the person who creates the course and invites others to view recordings.

Student: anyone who views Tegrity recordings.
